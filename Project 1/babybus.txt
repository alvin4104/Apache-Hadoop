BabyBus is a Chinese YouTube Channel and Web Series and brand that creates educational content for kids. Their videos are made to educate children to think independently, develop self-confidence, respect others, and explore the world. BabyBus also creates educational games for Android and iOS devices. The channel's mascots are Kiki and Miumiu. The brand is also known for creating the web series Safety Sheriff Labrador. Since their founding in 2009[5], it has became one of the most succesful kids brands, with over 120 million subscribers combined in all their YouTube channels and over 100 million monthly users accross the globe, most of them being between the ages of months and 8 years old, and even with thousands of videos uploaded on their respective YouTube channels. The only animated series that was produced was BabyBus: Magical Chinese Characters.
